## What We Need From You

To build an accurate forecasting system that reduces your food wastage by 50-70%, we need the following data:

| Data Needed | How Many Days | What Should Be in the Data |
|-------------|---------------|----------------------------|
| **Daily Sales Records** | Minimum 180 days (6 months)<br>Best: 365 days (1 year) | • Date and time of each order<br>• What dishes were ordered<br>• How many of each dish<br>• Dine-in or takeaway<br>• Total bill amount<br>• Whether order was completed or cancelled |
| **Menu List** | Current menu only | • Name of each dish<br>• Category (Burger, Sandwich, Wrap, etc.)<br>• Selling price<br>• Veg or Non-veg |
| **Recipe Details**<br>**(Most Important!)** | Current recipes | **For EACH dish on your menu, list:**<br>• What ingredients are needed<br>• How much of each ingredient (grams/pieces/ml)<br><br>Example: Chicken Burger needs:<br>- Chicken Breast: 140 grams<br>- Burger Bun: 1 piece<br>- Lettuce: 20 grams<br>- Tomato: 25 grams<br>- Cheese: 1 slice |
| **Raw Material Inventory List** | Current stock | • Name of each ingredient you buy<br>• How you measure it (grams/kg/pieces/liters)<br>• Price per unit<br>• How many days it lasts (shelf life)<br>• How many days for supplier delivery<br>• Current stock quantity |
| **Purchase/Restock History** | Last 180 days (6 months)<br>Best: 365 days (1 year) | • Date you bought ingredients<br>• Which ingredient/item<br>• Quantity purchased<br>• Total amount paid<br>• Supplier name<br>• When you ordered vs when you received<br><br>*(From your purchase bills/invoices)* |
| **Promotions & Offers**<br>**(If applicable)** | Last 180 days (6 months) | • Date of promotion<br>• What offer you ran (50% off, Buy 1 Get 1, etc.)<br>• Which dishes were on offer<br>• Any special events (festival specials, lunch combos)<br><br>*(Only if you ran promotions)* |
| **Wastage Records**<br>**(If you track it)** | Whatever history you have | • Date<br>• Which ingredient was wasted<br>• How much quantity<br>• Why it was wasted (expired/over-prepared/spoiled)<br>• Approximate cost<br><br>*(Only if you already maintain wastage logs)* |
| **Stock-out Records**<br>**(If you track it)** | Whatever history you have | • Date and time you ran out<br>• Which ingredient<br>• How many orders you couldn't fulfill<br>• Estimated money lost<br><br>*(Only if you remember or have records)* |
